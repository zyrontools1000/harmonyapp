Understanding Biblical Prosperity
Biblical prosperity is about much more than money. True prosperity includes:
  • Peace of mind
  • Financial stability
  • Opportunities and favor
  • Healthy relationships
  • Purpose and fulfillment
  • Generosity and stewardship
  • Confidence in God's provision
Prosperity begins internally before it manifests externally.
Prosperity is not merely about accumulating wealth. It is about living with confidence, purpose, peace, and trust in God's provision.
