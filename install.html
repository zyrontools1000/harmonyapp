How To Install the App
No app store needed. Harmony App is a smart PWA — it installs directly from your browser in seconds, works like a native app, and is always up to date automatically.
1
Open Chrome
Open the Chrome browser on your Android device.
2
Access Your Portal
Go to harmonyapp-sand.vercel.app and sign in.
3
Tap the Menu
In the top-right corner, tap the three-dot icon (⋮).
4
Add to Home Screen
Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
5
Confirm
Tap "Add" in the popup that appears.
✓
You're In!
Harmony App now appears on your home screen like a native app.
1
Open Safari
Open Safari on your iPhone — Chrome won't work for this step.
2
Access Your Portal
Type harmonyapp-sand.vercel.app and sign in.
3
Tap the Share Button
At the bottom, tap the Share icon ⬆︎ (box with arrow).
4
Add to Home Screen
Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen".
5
Confirm
Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
✓
You're In!
Harmony App is now on your home screen — tap anytime to enter.
