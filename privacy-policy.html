Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

1. Who We Are

HarmonyApp ("we," "us," or "our") is a spiritual wellness application providing Christian-inspired audio content, sacred frequencies, and guided reflection experiences. We are operated by Zampier Co. Ltda.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@harmonyapp.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide you with the HarmonyApp experience:

  • Account information: Your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Subscription status: Whether your account has an active subscription
  • Usage data: Pages visited within the app, for service improvement purposes
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device type for technical compatibility

We do not collect your payment information directly. All payments are processed by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Paddle's privacy policy governs the handling of your billing data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your subscription and grant access to content
  • To send you service-related communications (account confirmations, billing notices)
  • To improve the app experience based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

HarmonyApp uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication infrastructure
  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management (Merchant of Record)
  • Bunny.net — Content delivery network for audio and video files
  • Vercel — Application hosting and delivery

Each of these providers maintains their own privacy policies and security practices. We select partners who meet high standards of data protection.

5. Cookies

HarmonyApp uses minimal cookies strictly necessary to maintain your login session. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may prevent you from logging into the app.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@harmonyapp.app. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal data we collect, to opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell personal data), and to non-discriminatory treatment when exercising your rights.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of our subscription contract with you. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. If you cancel your subscription and request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

9. Children's Privacy

HarmonyApp is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice within the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
