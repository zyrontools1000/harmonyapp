Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🛡️

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with HarmonyApp within your first 30 days, we will refund your payment in full — no questions asked.

1. Our Commitment

We believe in HarmonyApp and want every member to feel confident joining. That's why we offer a straightforward 30-day satisfaction guarantee for all new subscribers.

We want you to experience the peace and spiritual depth of HarmonyApp — and if it's not right for you, we'll make it right.

2. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of your initial subscription payment
  • Applies to first-time subscribers only (one refund per account)
  • Applies to the most recent monthly charge if requested within 30 days of that charge
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method

3. How to Request a Refund

1

Send Us an Email

Email support@harmonyapp.app with the subject line: "Refund Request"

2

Include Your Details

Provide the email address used for your HarmonyApp account and the approximate date of your charge.

3

We Process Within 5 Business Days

Once approved, your refund will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

4. Subscription Cancellation

Canceling your subscription stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund. To cancel:

  • Email support@harmonyapp.app with subject "Cancel Subscription"
  • Or manage your subscription directly via your Paddle billing portal (link in your confirmation email)

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access until that date.

5. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We are committed to resolving any billing issues quickly and fairly. Chargebacks initiated without contacting support first may result in account suspension.

6. Contact

  • Email: support@harmonyapp.app
  • Response time: within 48 business hours
