🛡️ Day 3 – Shielding Against Envy and Negative Forces

📖 Daily Guide
Your light protects — but it also attracts. Today, you build your spiritual shield. Jealousy, judgment, unseen attacks, and spiritual interference lose access. You are no longer exposed. Today, Michael teaches you to hold what is yours — and reject what is not.
✦ Intention for the Day
"Nothing enters without my permission. I am protected, sealed, and at peace."
Before Listening
Sit tall. Breathe with intention. As the words unfold, visualize a sacred shield forming around you — not out of fear, but authority. When the audio ends, scroll to the spiritual action and complete the activation of your personal shield.
✨ Spiritual Action of the Day
Light a candle (white or blue, if available). Sit in front of it for a few moments, breathing slowly. As you gaze at the flame, visualize it forming a protective shield around you. When ready, say aloud three times: "I am shielded. No harm may enter." Then, before going to sleep tonight, write the affirmation on a small piece of paper and place it under your pillow.
✦ Affirmation
"What is true in me remains. All else dissolves before the light."
