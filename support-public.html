Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the HarmonyApp?

After subscribing, visit my.harmonyapp.app on your mobile browser and sign in with the email you used at checkout. For the best experience, add it to your home screen when prompted — it works like a native app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by emailing us at support@harmonyapp.app with the subject "Cancel Subscription." We'll process your cancellation within 24 hours and confirm by email. You will not be charged after cancellation.

How do I request a refund?

We offer full refunds — no questions asked. Simply email support@harmonyapp.app with the subject "Refund Request" and your purchase details. Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days.

What is included in my subscription?

Your $4.99/month membership gives you full access to all content in HarmonyApp — including Biblical Healing Frequencies, Sacred Resonance video sessions, Spiritual Scrolls, Morning Activation Series, Family Healing Prayers, Bedtime Stories, and all future content added to the library.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page at my.harmonyapp.app, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or email us for assistance.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We never store your payment details. All payments are processed securely by Paddle or Stripe — both PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. Your financial data is handled entirely by them, not by us.
